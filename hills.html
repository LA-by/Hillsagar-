Hill Station FAQ

Quick answers to help you decide when and where to go.

What is the best time to visit?

For the Himalayas (HP, UK, J&K, Sikkim, Arunachal) — Oct to Jun is pleasant; Dec–Feb for snow. For Western/Eastern Ghats (Kerala, TN, KA, MH) — Sep to May is ideal; monsoon is lush but brings heavier showers and occasional closures.

How do I get around?

Nearest airports connect to major hubs, with scenic road transfers. Some have toy trains (e.g., Darjeeling, Ooty). Always check local advisories during snow or heavy rain.

Any photography tips?

Golden hour (sunrise & sunset) enhances valleys and tea gardens. Carry a microfiber cloth (mist), ND filter for waterfalls, and a rain cover in monsoon.
