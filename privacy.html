Privacy Policy

At HillSagar, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our website.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal details (name, email, phone number) when you contact us.
  • Non-personal data such as browser type, device information, and website usage statistics.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to:

  • Respond to your inquiries and provide customer support.
  • Improve our website and user experience.
  • Send occasional updates, newsletters, or promotional offers (if subscribed).

3. Data Protection

We use appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data against unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure.

We do not sell or rent your personal data. However, we may share your information with trusted third-party services that help us operate our website (e.g., analytics, hosting providers).

Our website may use cookies to enhance user experience. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but some features of the website may not work properly.

You have the right to:

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The latest version will always be available on this page.
