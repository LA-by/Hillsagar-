12 Popular Indian Beaches

Radhanagar Beach

Often rated among Asia's best beaches, located in Havelock Island, Andaman and Nicobar Islands.

Andaman and Nicobar Islands

Palolem Beach

A serene crescent-shaped beach in South Goa known for its calm waters and stunning sunsets.

Goa

Kovalam Beach

Famous for its lighthouse and crescent-shaped coastline, located in Kerala.

Kerala

Varkala Beach

Unique cliff-beach combination with mineral springs and stunning views in Kerala.

Kerala

Agonda Beach

A quiet, pristine beach in South Goa perfect for relaxation and turtle spotting.

Goa

Marina Beach

One of the longest urban beaches in the world, located in Chennai, Tamil Nadu.

Tamil Nadu

Tarkarli Beach

Famous for its clear emerald waters and water sports activities in Maharashtra.

Maharashtra

Puri Beach

A sacred beach in Odisha known for its religious significance and beautiful sunrises.

Odisha

Dhanushkodi Beach

A ghost town beach at the southern tip of India where two oceans meet in Tamil Nadu.

Tamil Nadu

Baga Beach

One of Goa's most popular beaches known for its vibrant nightlife and water sports.

Goa

Kanyakumari Beach

Where the Arabian Sea, Bay of Bengal, and Indian Ocean meet at India's southernmost point.

Tamil Nadu

Gokarna Beach

A blend of spiritual serenity and beach paradise in Karnataka with several beautiful beaches.

Karnataka
